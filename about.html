About Liquor Haven

We are passionate about delivering the best selection of liquors to our valued customers.

Who We Are

Founded in 2010, Liquor Haven began as a small boutique spirits shop with a passion for quality and craftsmanship. What started as a humble storefront has grown into a premier destination for spirit enthusiasts, offering carefully curated selections from around the globe.

Our Mission

We believe that great spirits should be accessible to everyone. Our mission is to bridge the gap between distillers and consumers, bringing exceptional products to your doorstep while educating our customers about the art and tradition behind each bottle.

Our Team

Our team consists of certified sommeliers, spirits experts, and passionate individuals who taste and evaluate every product we carry. We travel to distilleries worldwide to establish direct relationships with producers, ensuring authenticity and quality.
